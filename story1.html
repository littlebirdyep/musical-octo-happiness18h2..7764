Gollum- an alternate possibility


A great commotion came from the back door. Something most peculiar had barged in and only seconds after disappeared, frightening everyone. Though none of the goblins knew why this was occuring, on the other side of the door and in the halls there was one who did. This was none other than Gollum, an entity that had been a resident of the underground caves since, well, no one knows when- maybe not even Gollum.
Ever since he had received a ring of power for his birthday, Gollum had cherished it, keeping it close until it drove him mad. For this was a special ring. It was valuable. It made you invisible and let Gollum eat goblin flesh for years. This thief, Bilbo Baggins the hobbit, had stolen Gollum's ring. His precious. Now it was gone, and the goblins were not alone in their hatred for Mr. Baggins.
We have little to no conclusive evidence that Gollum had warm blood, but as of now it was boiling with his sheer rage.
A while ago, Gollum had let loose a shriek as the thing he valued most in the world had left him on the hand of a little hobbit, and now he was planning to get his revenge as soon as he could.
We figures we should kill it when we sees ittt, preciousss... Gollum schemed quietly in the hallway. As soon as we gets back from the goblinses, yess...let us slit its throat most quietly and tear its fleshh from its limbsss. it shan't gets away for stealling my precious...Gollum!
Unfortunately, Gollum had a habit of talking to himself, and a goblin had heard him while he was too busy scheming to notice.
"What's this?" it murmured. "Is there something there? I should kill it."
Suddenly, it saw Gollum.
"Agh!!! What is that thing?!" it exclaimed in fright, alerting several other goblins.
"Kill it," The wicked folk cried. "Kill it right now before it kills us!""
Try he did, but Gollum was no match for the throng sent against him. He barrelled through the halls at top speed, but the goblins continued to pursue him. By the time he reached his island, he had killed many goblins, but in the end he was overpowered.
"Curse us and crush us," Gollum yelped. "Without the preciousss, we's as good as dead!"
