The Hobbit by JRR Tolkien


Hello to everyone reading this!
This website is a humble site about Tolkien's novel
The Hobbit!
The novel, as you most likely know, follows the story of
the unadventurous Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit from Bag-end.
Throughout the story, Mr. Baggins sees much change. He becomes
braver and takes risks, but he also makes many friends.

If you are looking to read one of Tolkien's novels, perhaps you would
like to read The Hobbit! Tolkien was in my mind a bit of a pioneer
of his time, so I really do hope you read at least one of his novels.
That will be all from me...for now! Farewell until we meet again...
