Hello to everyone reading this!
This right here is a simple site about Tolkien's novel
The Hobbit!
The novel, as you most likely know, follows the story of
the unadventurous Bilbo Baggins, a hobbit from Bag-end.
Throughout the story, Mr. Baggins changes quite a bit. Bilbo
doesn't merely get braver and take risks, he also makes many friends.

If you are looking to read one of Tolkien's novels, perhaps you would
like to read The Hobbit! Tolkien is in my mind a bit of a pioneer
of his time (take his interpretation of elves as an example!), so I
really do hope you read at least one of his novels if you haven't.
That will be all from me...for now! Farewell until we meet again...
